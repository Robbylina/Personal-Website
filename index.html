About me

Hi, I'm Alina!

I am an aspiring professional in sustainability, people & culture and data. I am convinced that we need data-driven insights and people as the agents of change to drive the sustainable transition that is at the heart of almost all of mankind's challenges. With my Master Degree in Business Administration within the subject Entrepreneurship, Sustainability and Social Change and my work experience in Human Resources, Diversity, Recruiting and Talent Management, I am on a mission to bring relationships, communication and data together to shape holistic sustainable change in society and organisations.

Let's work together for a sustainable present and future!

Professional skills

Sustainability

I perceive sustainability holistically from a systems and context perspective. Having studied the intersection of environmental, social, political and economic sustainability aspects at Master's niveau made me proficient in various sustainability tools and frameworks.

People & Culture

People are the agents of change. With my working experience in managing talent, knowledge and diversity, I facilitate empowerment and commitment to sustainability through building relationships and leveraging meaningful communication.

Data & Technology

We need data-driven insights and technology for informed and effective action. With my skills in qualitative and quantitative data management and analysis as well as programming, I constantly explore technological advancements that foster sustainable change.
